FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What are your operating hours?

We're open 7 days a week from 5:00 AM to 10:00 PM, including all holidays. We believe fitness doesn't take a day off!

Do I need to bring my own lock for the locker?

Yes, please bring your own lock for security. We also sell high-quality locks at our reception if you need one.

Is parking available?

Yes, we have dedicated parking space for our members right outside the gym premises, monitored by CCTV for security.

Can I freeze my membership?

Yes, memberships can be frozen for medical reasons with proper documentation. Please speak to our staff for details.

Do you provide personal training?

Yes, we offer personal training sessions with certified trainers. You can book individual sessions or packages at discounted rates.
